# VAR-and-ES-Analysis-for-Multi-Index-Indian-Market-Portfolio
• Implemented Historical, Parametric and Monte Carlo Value at Risk (VaR) methodologies for a portfolio comprising NIFTY Midcap
100, NIFTY Bank, NIFTY IT and NIFTY FMCG indices, analyzing 13 years of market data to compare risk estimation accuracy.

• Integrated Expected Shortfall (ES) calculations and conducted backtesting using the Kupiec test and Basel III Traffic. Light
Approach, revealing 98.97% accuracy for Historical VaR and identifying risk underestimation in Parametric and Monte Carlo.

• Performed stress testing to assess portfolio resilience under extreme market conditions, demonstrating a 19.18% portfolio value at risk
during simulated market downturns, enhancing understanding of tail risk across diverse Indian market sectors.
